Recipe for menemen

Recipe

Menemen are turkish scrambled eggs with fresh tomatoes and peppers. This dish is usually consumed at breakfast with some bread. Nevertheless, it is suitable for any meal.

I have just tried recipe with some corn tortilla for dinner. It was very satisfaying. I hope you like it.

Ingredients for 1 large serving

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 large white onion
  • 10 grams of olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Step

  1. Wash the vegetables
  2. Dice the onion
  3. Put the oil to heat in a large pane
  4. Cook the onion under low heat for 10 minutes sturring regularly
  5. Dice the pepper
  6. Peel and blend the tomatoes
  7. Scramble the 3 eggs and season them to taste
  8. After the 10 minutes add the vegetables to the pot, season to taste and cook under low heat for 10 minutes
  9. Add the scrambled eggs and continue cooking until the desired consistancy is achieved
  10. Serve in a bowl
